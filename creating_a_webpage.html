You can only use the services of one mobile carrier on a single SIM device.
Dual SIM phones provide more options depending on the number of SIM cards installed. On a dual SIM device, you can set default SIM cards for making calls and using mobile data in Settings > SIM cards & mobile networks. You can edit SIM card names which are displayed on the dial pad. To do this, go to Settings > SIM cards & mobile networks and tap your SIM card. Options for changing preferred network types are available in Settings > SIM cards & mobile networks > Preferred network type
